### Q1. Priority Scale

Three-tier scale, not High/Medium/Low or numeric:

| Tier | Meaning | Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| CRITICAL | Immediate management attention required | Work is significantly delayed or not completed, staff performance is below expectations, and output is not meeting required targets. Immediate action and follow-up are needed. |
| AMBER | Needs monitoring and improvement | Work is partially completed or at risk of delay, staff performance is average or inconsistent, and output is below expected standard but recoverable with follow-up. |
| GREEN | On track / Performing well | Work is completed on time, staff performance meets or exceeds expectations, and output is achieved according to required quality and productivity standards. |
