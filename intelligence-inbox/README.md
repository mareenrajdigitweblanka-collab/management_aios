## Purpose

This folder is the intake area for raw, unprocessed intelligence relevant to the LEDSone Management AIOS. Items placed here are collected observations, notes, and signals that have not yet been reviewed, validated, or approved for use in the system.

Nothing in this folder should be treated as verified truth until it has been reviewed by Varmen and moved to an appropriate approved location.

---

## Subfolders

- `recurring-issues/` — Patterns of recurring problems or friction points observed in the management context.
- `stakeholder-interview-notes/` — Raw notes from stakeholder interviews, saved after interviews are conducted.
- `processed/` — Items that have been reviewed and processed (filed, approved, or discarded).

## What Belongs Here

- Raw, unprocessed notes and observations.
- Interview notes saved after interviews are completed (not before).
- Observed patterns awaiting review.

## What Must NOT Be Stored Here Yet

- Verified or approved facts (those go to `../context/` after approval).
- Sensitive personal HR data (names, salaries, performance records).
- Any content invented without a real source.
- Approved decisions (those go to `../decisions/`).
