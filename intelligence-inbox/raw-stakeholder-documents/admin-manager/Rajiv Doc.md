# GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK

# **DIGIT WEB LANKA GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK**

## **1\. Purpose**

This document defines how Digit Web Lanka (Pvt) Ltd is governed, managed, monitored, and controlled. It establishes organizational structure, authority levels, reporting relationships, escalation mechanisms, operational controls, and accountability systems to ensure consistent execution and organizational growth.

# **13\. Management Drift Assessment**

The following areas require continuous monitoring:

## **Potential Drift Area 1**

Verbal instructions replacing documented procedures.

Risk: Loss of consistency and accountability.

## **Potential Drift Area 2**

Knowledge concentrated within specific employees.

Risk: Operational disruption when key employees leave.

## **Potential Drift Area 3**

Projects started without approved requirements.

Risk: Scope creep and wasted resources.

## **Potential Drift Area 4**

Inconsistent KPI follow-up.

Risk: Lack of accountability.

## **Potential Drift Area 5**

Escalation bypassing.

Risk: Management overload and confusion.

## **Potential Drift Area 6**

SOP non-compliance.

Risk: Operational inconsistency.

## **Potential Drift Area 7**

Unclear ownership between departments.

Risk: Tasks falling between teams.

## **Potential Drift Area 8**

Insufficient documentation.

Risk: Dependency on memory and verbal explanations.

# Responsibilities

### **Admin Manager Responsibilities**

#### **Administrative Management**

* Oversee and manage administrative operations across the company.  
* Ensure compliance with company policies, procedures, and standards.  
* Maintain organisational discipline, accountability, and operational efficiency.

#### **SOP and Process Management**

* Develop, maintain, and enforce Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).  
* Ensure business processes, policies, and knowledge are properly documented.  
* Build systems that enable new employees to understand and perform their roles efficiently.

#### **Manage By Exception (MBE)**

* Focus on significant issues, exceptions, and strategic decisions rather than routine operational matters.  
* Empower teams to work independently within established systems.  
* Escalate and resolve critical issues when necessary.

#### **Performance Monitoring**

* Monitor employee and departmental performance.  
* Support the implementation of ROI-based performance measurement systems.  
* Assist management in evaluating productivity and effectiveness.

#### **Development Team Governance**

* Ensure software development activities are properly documented.  
* Verify that requirements, workflows, and technical knowledge are not dependent on individual employees.  
* Promote AI-assisted review and governance through structured documentation.

#### **Infrastructure and Compliance**

* Review infrastructure-related documentation, including servers, backups, storage, power management, and governance.  
* Identify gaps between requirements and implementation.  
* Follow up with the responsible parties to close identified gaps.

#### **Training and Knowledge Management**

* Develop training systems for new employees.  
* Convert individual knowledge into organizational knowledge.  
* Reduce dependency on specific employees through documentation and structured processes.

#### **Cross-Department Coordination**

* Coordinate with the Managing Director, Operations Team, Development Team, and other departments.  
* Ensure requirements are clearly defined before projects begin.  
* Monitor alignment between operational activities and company objectives.

#### **Core Objective**

* Build a structured, measurable, documented, and scalable organisation that does not depend on individual employees for critical knowledge or operations.

---

### **KPI Meetings and Staff Management Responsibilities**

#### **KPI Meeting Management**

* Collect KPI reports from all departments.  
* Verify the accuracy and completeness of KPI data.  
* Organize and coordinate KPI meetings.  
* Ensure Team Leaders and Department Heads submit KPI reports on time.  
* Identify areas where KPI targets are not achieved.  
* Facilitate root cause analysis for underperformance.  
* Ensure corrective action plans are developed and implemented.  
* Report KPI outcomes to the Managing Director and management team.  
* Record action items arising from KPI meetings.  
* Follow up on agreed actions and monitor progress.  
* Track KPI performance on a weekly and monthly basis.

#### **Staff Management**

##### **Attendance and Discipline**

* Monitor employee attendance and punctuality.  
* Handle disciplinary matters and policy violations.  
* Maintain disciplinary records, warning letters, and related documentation.

##### 

##### **Performance Management**

* Evaluate employee performance.  
* Identify underperforming employees and teams.  
* Monitor performance improvement actions.  
* Support ROI and output-based performance assessment systems.

##### **Staff Development**

* Identify employee training needs.  
* Monitor onboarding and integration of new employees.

##### **Staff Accountability**

* Ensure every employee has clearly defined responsibilities.  
* Monitor completion of assigned tasks.  
* Follow up on pending tasks, unresolved issues, and commitments.

##### **Recruitment and Staffing Support**

* Assess staffing requirements.  
* Support recruitment, interviewing, and selection processes.  
* Monitor the successful integration of new hires into the organization.

#### **Management Follow-Up**

* Prepare KPI meeting minutes and action reports.  
* Maintain staff issue registers.  
* Maintain action trackers for management decisions.  
* Monitor department-wise pending issues.  
* Ensure management decisions are implemented and completed as agreed.
