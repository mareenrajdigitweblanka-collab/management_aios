## Purpose

This folder holds items from the intelligence inbox that have been reviewed and processed. Processed means: the item has been read, evaluated by Varmen, and either approved for promotion to another folder, filed for reference, or formally discarded. Items moved here are closed intake items.

## What Belongs Here

- Raw intake items (interview notes, recurring issue observations) that have been reviewed by Varmen.
- A brief processing note should accompany each item: outcome (promoted / filed / discarded), date reviewed, and reviewer.

## What Must NOT Be Stored Here Yet

- Active or pending intelligence items (those stay in sibling folders until reviewed).
- Sensitive personal data beyond what was already authorised in the source item.
- Any invented content.
