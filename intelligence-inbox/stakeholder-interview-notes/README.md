## Purpose

This folder is the designated location for raw notes taken during stakeholder interviews. Notes are saved here after interviews occur. They are raw and unverified until reviewed by Varmen.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Raw notes from completed stakeholder interviews.
- Each note file should record: date, interviewee role (not personal name unless authorised), key points raised, and any uncertainties flagged.
- Notes saved here are intake material only — not approved truth.

## What Must NOT Be Stored Here Yet

- Notes created before interviews have taken place.
- Invented or assumed stakeholder statements.
- Sensitive personal data (full names, salaries, performance ratings) unless explicitly authorised.
- Approved facts or verified context (those go to `../../../context/` after Varmen review).

---

## Evidence / Validation Note

Raw interview notes must not be promoted to context files without Varmen review. Each note should be traceable to a specific interview event. Approval must be recorded in `../../../evidence/` before any note is used as a source for context or skill files.
