## Purpose

This folder holds handover notes prepared when an intern or contributor passes ownership of a task, phase, or the system as a whole to Varmen or another authorised owner. Handover notes ensure continuity, record what was done, what was left incomplete, and what decisions are pending.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Intern-to-owner handover notes at the end of a work phase.
- Closure notes when a phase is formally completed.
- Summary of work done, decisions pending, open items, and recommended next steps.
- Context required for Varmen to continue work without needing to re-investigate.

## What Must NOT Be Stored Here Yet

- Sensitive personal HR data (employee details, performance records).
- Invented or assumed status updates.
- Handover notes created before the work phase is actually complete.
- Notes that treat unverified facts as confirmed.

---

## Evidence / Validation Note

Each handover note should reference relevant items in `../evidence/`, `../decisions/`, and `../validation/` so Varmen can trace the basis for any claim or status reported in the handover. Handover notes are not approvals — they inform the approver.
