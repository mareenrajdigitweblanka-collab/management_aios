## Policy Record 2 — Leave Without Notice (Unplanned / Unannounced)

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Policy Topic** | Unplanned leave and leave taken without prior notice |
| **Source Section** | SRC-POLICY-001 §6.0, §6.3 |
| **Plain-Language Explanation** | All leave — including unplanned leave — requires the leave system to be updated at least 48 hours in advance (§6.0). For unplanned leave, HR or the supervisor must be notified as soon as possible (§6.3). Staff are entitled to 5 unplanned leave days per year. Leave taken with no notification at all — where neither the leave system has been updated nor has HR or a supervisor been informed — is treated as unauthorised leave. Unauthorised leave may lead to disciplinary action. |
| **What Is Allowed** | Up to 5 unplanned leave days per year, with HR or supervisor notified as soon as possible. |
| **What Is Not Allowed** | Taking leave without updating the leave system; taking leave without notifying HR or a supervisor; exceeding 5 unplanned leave days without authorisation. Leave taken with no communication whatsoever is unauthorised leave. |
| **Reviewer Needed** | HR Officer (Mayurika) — leave compliance and record authority (SRC-MAYU-001). Supervisor or Team Leader for immediate notification and verification of circumstances. |
| **[VERIFY]** | None — this policy area is fully confirmed by SRC-POLICY-001 §6.0 and §6.3. |
