## Purpose

This folder holds validation checklists and review outputs used to confirm that system components meet defined standards before being approved. Checklists here are tools for structured review — they are not approvals themselves. Once a checklist is completed and signed off by Varmen, the approval record goes to `../decisions/` and the evidence goes to `../evidence/`.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Validation checklists for skeleton builds.
- Validation checklists for context file reviews.
- Validation checklists for skill file reviews.
- Review output documents (e.g., pass/fail summaries from structured reviews).
- Checklist templates for future use.

## What Must NOT Be Stored Here Yet

- Completed checklists treated as approvals without Varmen sign-off.
- Sensitive personal HR data.
- Invented or pre-filled checklists.
- Live system outputs or integration data.

---

## Evidence / Validation Note

Completed checklists must be reviewed by Varmen. The outcome (PASS/FAIL + notes) should be recorded here, with a corresponding record in `../decisions/` for any approval made, and in `../evidence/` for the audit trail.
