# NSLP Before & After Evidence Template

**Source:** SRC-MAYURIKA-NSLP-001 — HR.Mayu.Skill.md Section 9

**Purpose:** Records Before and After conditions for each NSLP participant. HR uses this to verify that the skill was applied and that measurable improvement occurred. Opinion-only evidence is not accepted.

**Safety note:** No real staff names, employee IDs, salary, health, grievance, disciplinary, KPI individual scores, or PDPA personal data. Use role/placeholder references only.

**Root Truth Rule:** If this template conflicts with HR.Mayu.Skill.md Section 9, the skill file is correct.
