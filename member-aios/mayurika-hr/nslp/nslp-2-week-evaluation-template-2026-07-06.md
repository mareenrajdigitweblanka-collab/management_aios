## Pass/Fail at Evaluation

**PASS if:**
- Skill applied continuously
- Measurable proof provided
- Before & After evidence both reviewed
- Improvement sustained
- Outcome label assigned

**FAIL if:**
- Skill not applied
- No measurable proof
- Opinion-only evidence
- Improvement not sustained

**Evaluation result:** PASS / FAIL / PENDING
