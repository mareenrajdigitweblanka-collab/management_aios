# NSLP Monthly Management Report — Template

**Source:** SRC-MAYURIKA-NSLP-001 — HR.Mayu.Skill.md Section 9

**Purpose:** Monthly evidence-backed report to management on NSLP implementation status, adoption rate, business impact, exceptions, and recommendations. Management reports must include evidence — opinion-only summaries are not accepted.

**Safety note:** No real staff names, employee IDs, salary, health, grievance, disciplinary, KPI individual scores, or PDPA personal data. Use aggregate figures and role/placeholder references only.

**Root Truth Rule:** If this template conflicts with HR.Mayu.Skill.md Section 9, the skill file is correct.
