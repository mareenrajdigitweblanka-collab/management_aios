## Purpose

This folder holds formally approved decision records for the LEDSone Management AIOS. A decision record documents a significant choice made about the system's design, scope, content, or operation — and records the rationale, who approved it, and when.

---

## What Belongs Here

- Approved decision records only.
- Each record should include: decision title, date, decision-maker (Varmen or nominated approver), rationale, and outcome.
- Examples of future approved decisions:
  - Approval to populate `CLAUDE.md`
  - Approval to create specific context files
  - Approval to build specific skill files
  - Approval to connect a live integration

## What Must NOT Be Stored Here Yet

- Draft or unapproved decisions.
- Decisions made without Varmen sign-off.
- HR or management policy decisions not yet approved.
- Sensitive personal employee data.
- Invented or assumed decisions.

---

## Evidence / Validation Note

Every decision record here must have a corresponding evidence item in `../evidence/`. No decision should be filed here without a traceable review record.
